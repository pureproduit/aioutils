Frequently asked questions

Do I need a credit card for the free plan?
No. The free plan requires no account and no credit card. Just visit the tool and start using it.
How does the daily limit work?
Free users get 10 AI generations per day, reset at midnight. This applies across all tools combined.
Can I cancel my Pro subscription anytime?
Yes, absolutely. Cancel anytime from your account settings. You keep Pro access until the end of your billing period.
Is my data private?
Yes. We do not store or log your text inputs. Your content is sent to the AI model and returned to you — that's it.
What AI model powers the tools?
We use OpenAI's GPT-4o mini model for fast, high-quality results. Pro users will get access to GPT-4o (the more powerful version) soon.
